30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our courses. If you're not completely satisfied within 30 days of purchase, we'll refund your payment—no questions asked.

1. Our Commitment to You

At BraveFx, we're confident in the value and quality of our educational content. We want you to feel secure in your investment. That's why we offer a straightforward 30-day money-back guarantee on all course purchases.

If for any reason you're not satisfied with your course purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date—no questions asked.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

Within 30 Days

Your refund request is submitted within 30 calendar days of your original purchase date

First-Time Purchase

This is your first purchase of the specific course

Valid Account

Your account is in good standing with no violations of our Terms and Conditions

Original Payment Method

Refund will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase

3. Refund Exclusions

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following circumstances:

After 30 Days

Refund requests submitted more than 30 days after purchase

Repeat Purchases

Re-purchasing a course you previously bought and refunded

Terms Violations

Violation of our Terms and Conditions, including content sharing or account abuse

Excessive Downloads

Downloading substantially all course materials before requesting a refund

Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute instead of following our refund process

Promotional Offers

Courses received for free through promotions or scholarships (unless otherwise stated)

4. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

1

Contact Support

Send an email to academy@bravefx.io with the subject line "Refund Request"

2

Provide Information

Include your full name, email address associated with your account, course name, and purchase date (if available)

3

Wait for Confirmation

We'll review your request and respond within 1-2 business days with confirmation

4

Receive Your Refund

Once approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method

Email Template: "Hello, I would like to request a refund for [Course Name] purchased on [Date]. My registered email is [Your Email]. Thank you."

5. Refund Processing Timeline

Once your refund is approved, here's what to expect:

  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days from approval
  • Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase
  • Bank Processing: Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • Total Time: You should expect to see your refund within 10-14 business days from approval

Request Received → Review (1-2 days) → Processing (5-7 days) → Bank Processing (3-5 days)

Total: Approximately 10-14 business days

6. What Happens After a Refund

Once your refund is processed:

  • Your access to the course will be immediately revoked
  • You will no longer be able to view course videos, materials, or resources
  • Your enrollment will be removed from your account
  • Any certificates earned will be invalidated
  • Downloaded materials should be deleted (per our Terms and Conditions)

You are welcome to repurchase the course in the future, but please note that repeat refund requests for the same course may be denied.

7. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds. Our refund policy is "all or nothing":

  • If you're eligible, you receive a 100% refund
  • We do not prorate refunds based on course completion percentage
  • We do not offer refunds for individual modules or lessons

8. Technical Issues and Support

Before requesting a refund due to technical issues, please:

  • Contact our support team at academy@bravefx.io
  • Provide details about the technical problem you're experiencing
  • Allow us 48 hours to resolve the issue

Most technical issues can be resolved quickly. We're here to help ensure you have the best learning experience possible.

9. Refund Abuse Prevention

We trust our students, but we must protect our platform from abuse. We reserve the right to:

  • Deny refund requests that appear fraudulent or abusive
  • Limit or revoke refund privileges for users with patterns of abuse
  • Request additional information to verify refund requests
  • Suspend or terminate accounts involved in refund fraud

Examples of refund abuse include: repeatedly purchasing and refunding courses, sharing course content before requesting a refund, or using multiple accounts to circumvent refund limits.

10. Chargebacks and Disputes

Important: Do Not Initiate Chargebacks

Please use our refund process instead of initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. Chargebacks:

  • Result in immediate account suspension
  • May result in denial of the chargeback (our records show purchase and access)
  • Prevent you from using BraveFx in the future
  • Take longer to process than our standard refund

If you have an issue, please contact us first at academy@bravefx.io. We're here to help and will work with you to resolve any concerns.

11. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Material changes will be communicated to users via email.

Note: Refund requests are governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase, not at the time of the refund request.

12. Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact us:

Email: academy@bravefx.io

Subject Line: "Refund Question" or "Refund Request"

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours (1-2 business days)
